UFIT MOTION

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Ufit Motion ("we," "us," "the platform") is operated by Ufit, a physical-education program-delivery company, to support PE coaching, student progress tracking, and program reporting at K–8 schools. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights of students, parents, and school staff.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data needed to deliver and report on PE programs:

  • Student records: first and last name, grade, school, optional gender (provided by the school), assessment scores, attendance, behavioral observations made during PE class.
  • Parent contact information: name, email address, and phone number, when a parent self-registers to view their child's progress.
  • Staff records: name, email, role, school assignment, and (for Ufit employees) employment-related fields such as training level and pay rate.
  • Operational data: session logs, end-of-day reports, incident reports, and audit logs of who took which action in the platform.

We do not collect Social Security numbers, financial account numbers, biometric data, or precise location data.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Deliver PE coaching services contracted by your school or school district.
  • Track and report student progress to authorized school staff and parents.
  • Maintain audit logs for safety and compliance investigations.
  • Communicate with school staff and parents about account access, password resets, and service updates.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use student data for advertising.

3. FERPA Compliance

Student records on this platform are "education records" under the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (20 U.S.C. § 1232g) and equivalent state laws. Ufit operates as a "school official" with a "legitimate educational interest" under each district's contract. We:

  • Use student data only for the educational purpose described in our contract with your school.
  • Limit access to authorized school officials and Ufit personnel assigned to that school.
  • Do not redisclose student data to any other party without the school's prior written consent or as required by law.
  • Honor parent requests to inspect, correct, or delete their child's records — submit requests to your school's designated FERPA officer; the school will route the request to us.

4. Data Retention

We retain student records for the duration of your school's contract with Ufit, plus a reasonable period after contract termination to meet legal and audit requirements (typically 3 years). Parents may request earlier deletion through their school. Audit logs are retained for at least 1 year.

5. Security

We protect data with industry-standard controls: HTTPS for all traffic, encrypted storage at the database layer, password hashing with PBKDF2-SHA-256, role-based access control scoped to the school or district, automated audit logging of every state-changing action, and routine backups. We restrict administrative access to a small number of authorized Ufit personnel. We will notify affected schools without undue delay if we become aware of a security incident affecting student data.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Students do not create accounts on this platform. All student data is provided by the school or by parents with school consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

If you are a parent, you may:

  • Request access to your child's records held in this platform — through your school's FERPA officer.
  • Request correction of inaccurate records.
  • Request deletion of your child's records, subject to the school's retention policy.
  • Withdraw consent for parent-portal access at any time.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the platform evolves. Material changes will be communicated to school administrators in advance.

9. Contact

Questions, concerns, or requests: operations@ufitonline.net.
