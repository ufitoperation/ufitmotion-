Training Manual

A click-by-click guide for every role on Ufit Motion. Hand the relevant chapter to a new staff member; they should be able to do their job after one read-through. Each chapter is self-contained — skip to yours.

Chapter 1

Admin

You're a Ufit operations administrator. You're the source of truth on school onboarding, coach hiring, and program compliance. This chapter walks you through the full admin workflow.

Logging in

1
Go to https://ufitmotion.onrender.com/login.
2
Click Admin Portal (the building icon, top-left).
3
Enter your email and password. Click Sign In.
4
If this is your first login, you arrived via an email link. Set your password (8+ characters) and click Save. You're then auto-redirected to the dashboard.
Forgot password? Click the "Forgot password?" link below the Sign In button. You'll receive a reset link within ~30 seconds at the email on file.

Adding a school

This is the first thing you do for a new client district.

1
From the dashboard, click the Schools item in the left sidebar.
2
Click + Add School in the top-right of the page.
3
In the modal: pick or create the parent organization (district), enter the school name, choose school type (Elementary, Middle, K-8, etc.), city, state.
4
Optional but recommended: add the principal's name + email. The system will auto-send a "Set My Password" invite to that email when you save.
5
Click Add School. The school appears in your schools table immediately, with a yellow ⏱ Pending badge next to the principal's name until they set their password.

Adding coaches

Three ways to onboard coaches. Pick the one that matches your situation.

Path 1 — One coach at a time

Use this when you have a single new hire and you have all their details on hand.

1
Sidebar → Coaches.
2
Click + Add Coach.
3
Fill in: first name, last name, email, role (Head Coach / Assistant / Site Coordinator / etc.), and the school they'll work at.
4
Leave the password field blank — the system generates a 24-hour invite token and emails the coach a "Set My Password" link.
5
Submit. The coach appears in your coaches table with ⏱ Pending until they click their email link.

Path 2 — Bulk invite (paste / CSV)

Use this when you're onboarding an entire school's coaching staff at once.

1
Sidebar → Coaches → click Bulk Invite (next to + Add Coach).
2
Set Default Role (used when a row leaves the role blank).
3
Set Default Schools (used when a row doesn't specify a school).
4
Paste rows in first,last,email,role format — one per line. Up to 200 rows per batch.
5
Click Send Invites. Server returns: X created · Y duplicates · Z errors with row numbers for any failures. Bad rows don't kill the batch.

Sample paste:

Ada,Lovelace,ada@lincoln.edu,head_coach
Bo,Knight,bo@lincoln.edu,
Cam,Reyes,cam@lincoln.edu,assistant_coach

Path 3 — School invite code (let coaches sign themselves up)

Use this when the school's HR is hiring directly and you don't yet know who's coming.

1
From the school's detail page, find the Coach Invite Code (e.g. LCN-X7M9-2026).
2
Hand the code to the school's HR contact. They share it with new coaches.
3
Coach goes to https://ufitmotion.onrender.com/login → Coach Portal → "Sign up with school code" → enters the code + their info.
4
You'll get a notification: "Coach X self-registered at Lincoln via invite code." Verify the person actually belongs there.
5
If the code leaks publicly, click Regenerate on the school detail page. The old code is dead immediately.

Importing students

1
Sidebar → Students.
2
Click Import CSV in the top-right.
3
Drop the school's roster CSV. Required columns: first, last, grade, local_student_identifier.
4
Preview shows the first 5 rows. Verify the column mapping is correct.
5
Click Confirm Import. Students appear in the roster immediately.
Always include local_student_identifier. This is the key parents use to verify their child during self-registration. Without it, parents have to know an internal integer ID, which most won't.

Monitoring activity

The dashboard is your daily check-in. Once schools are live, the empty-state checklist disappears and stat cards light up.

  • Schools / Coaches / Sessions / EOD compliance / Open incidents — five stat cards across the top.
  • Quick Actions — shortcuts to Add School, Add Coach, Review Incidents.
  • This Week at a Glance — sessions logged + EOD compliance rate.
  • 🔔 Bell icon — unread notifications (incident filed, parent registered, coach pending).

Reviewing incidents

1
Sidebar → Incidents.
2
Filter by status: Open / Investigating / Resolved / Closed. Default is Open.
3
Click any row to see the full detail: type, severity, description, action taken, school + family notification booleans.
4
Add resolution notes and update status as the case progresses.
Critical incidents auto-notify all admins. If a coach files an incident with severity = critical, the bell icon updates for every admin / CEO / overseer in the org within ~5 seconds.

Exporting reports (CSV)

Three CSV exports for sharing with school districts, board members, or your own spreadsheets.

WhatWhere
Schools listSidebar → Schools → Export CSV
Coaches listSidebar → Coaches → Export CSV
Students listSidebar → Students → Export CSV

Each downloads as a .csv file with a clear filename. Open in Excel, Google Sheets, or attach to email.

Editing a coach's role or schools

1
Sidebar → Coaches.
2
Click the Edit icon on the coach's row.
3
Change the role (Head Coach ↔ Assistant Coach, etc.) and/or the multi-school selection.
4
Save. The coach's old assignments are marked inactive; new assignments are created. Audit log row written automatically.
Permission rules: You cannot edit your own role here. Only the CEO can grant or demote admin / CEO roles. Admins can change any non-CEO/non-admin user.

Troubleshooting (admin)

SymptomLikely cause / fix
"Invalid email or password" at loginEither typo or your account was deactivated. Click "Forgot password" to reset.
Coach never received their invite emailCheck the email is correct, then click Resend Invite on the coach's row. The 24h token regenerates.
Bulk invite shows duplicatesEmail already in the system (live or soft-deleted). Use the existing user or contact support.
Can't add a school principal because the email existsThat email is already used by another user. Pick a different principal email or contact support.
Stat cards say 0 but I have dataThe dashboard caches for ~5 seconds. Refresh the page.
Chapter 2

Coach

You're a coach in the field. You're using this on a phone, outside, between activities. The app is built so every common task takes < 2 minutes.

Logging in

1
Open https://ufitmotion.onrender.com/login on your phone (Safari on iPhone, Chrome on Android both work).
2
Tap Coach Portal (the runner icon).
3
Enter your email + password. Tap Sign In.
4
First time? You arrived via an email link. Set a password (8+ characters), then proceed.

Pro tip: Add the page to your home screen — Safari → share → Add to Home Screen — for one-tap access. The app behaves like a native app from there.

Multi-school picker

If you're assigned to two or more schools, you'll see a "Where are you working today?" screen after login. Tap the school you're at right now.

Switching schools mid-day: tap the school chip in the top-nav (e.g. ● Lincoln Elementary ▾). The picker reopens. Pick the new school. Your dashboard updates without logging out.

Logging a session

Do this at the start or end of your session — either way is fine.

1
Sidebar (or hamburger menu on phone) → Sessions.
2
Tap + Log Session.
3
Pick the program (auto-filled from your assignment). Set the date (defaults to today). Set start + end times.
4
Choose session type: Regular / Makeup / Tournament / Assessment.
5
Briefly describe planned + actual activities (you can update actual at the end).
6
Tap Save. The session shows up in your sessions list.

Taking attendance

1
Open the session you just created.
2
Tap Take Attendance.
3
For each student, tap one: Present, Absent, Excused, Partial. Default is Present.
4
If you mark Partial, set participation level: Full / Moderate / Limited / Refused.
5
Tap Save. Total students present updates automatically.

Filing an EOD report

Required at the end of every session. Target time: under 2 minutes.

1
Sidebar → EOD Reports.
2
Tap + File EOD.
3
Required fields: Activities completed, Student engagement, Behavior summary, Success story, Challenge summary.
4
If anything went wrong: tap Injury / Incident occurred. This pre-fills an incident report after submit.
5
Optional site-level fields: school concerns, schedule changes, equipment requests, late arrivals, etc. Leave blank if nothing to report.
6
Tap Submit EOD. The compliance ✓ On-time badge appears if you submitted same day.
"Submitted on time" matters. EOD compliance % on the admin dashboard is calculated from this field. Submit your EODs before midnight Pacific same-day.

Scoring assessments

Use this during baseline (first 2-4 weeks), monthly progress checks, or quarterly reporting windows.

1
Sidebar → Assessments.
2
Tap + Score Student.
3
Pick the student from your roster. Skill catalog auto-filters to their grade band (K-2 / 3-5 / 6-8).
4
Pick the skill (Dribbling, Chest pass, Galloping, etc.).
5
Pick the level: 1 Emerging / 2 Developing / 3 Functional / 4 Proficient / 5 Advanced. Tap the corresponding card.
6
Required: pick an observation tag from the dropdown — independent / needs prompt / inconsistent / strong control / low confidence / gameplay transfer observed.
7
Optional: add a one-line note for context.
8
Tap Save Score. The normalized score (20 / 40 / 60 / 80 / 100) is computed automatically.

Filing an incident

Use this for injuries, behavior issues, equipment failures, facility problems.

1
Sidebar → Incidents.
2
Tap + File Incident.
3
Pick incident type: Injury / Behavior / Equipment / Facility / Safety / Other.
4
Pick severity: Low / Medium / High / Critical. Critical fires immediate admin notifications.
5
If a specific student is involved, pick them from the dropdown.
6
Describe what happened (description) and what you did about it (immediate action taken).
7
Toggle School notified + Family notified as appropriate. These are tracked separately for compliance.
8
Tap Submit Incident. An audit log row is written automatically.

Chapter 3

Principal / School Staff

You're a principal or designated school staff member. The platform is read-mostly for you — you review, monitor, and submit periodic feedback. Designed for a 30-second scan between bell rings.

Logging in

1
Open https://ufitmotion.onrender.com/login.
2
Click Organization Portal (the school icon).
3
Enter your email + password. First time? Use the "Set My Password" link from the email Ufit sent you.

Reviewing the dashboard

Your dashboard shows ONLY your school. You can't see other schools in the district.

  • Open incidents banner (red, top of page) — any unresolved injuries / behavior issues / facility problems.
  • EOD on-time % — what fraction of this week's sessions had EODs filed same-day.
  • Sessions logged — count for this week.
  • Active students — total enrolled in your PE program.
  • Latest EOD card — most recent end-of-day report from your school's coaches.

Reading an EOD

1
Sidebar → EOD Reports.
2
Reports are listed newest first. Click any row to expand.
3
You'll see: activities, engagement summary, behavior summary, success story, challenge, any flagged incidents, site-level concerns.
4
If a coach flagged "principal communication needed", the EOD will be highlighted at the top until you mark it reviewed.

Student progress

1
Sidebar → My Students.
2
Search or filter by grade level.
3
Click any student. You'll see baseline → current → growth across all assessed skills, behavior readiness ratings, and attendance %.

Filing a satisfaction survey

Quarterly. Ufit operations sends you a reminder.

1
Sidebar → Surveys.
2
Click + New Survey.
3
Rate overall satisfaction (1-5), program impact (1-5), communication quality (1-5). Add free-text suggestions.
4
Submit. Your responses go to the Ufit ops team but are NOT visible to the coaches you're rating.
Chapter 4

Parent

You're a parent. You want to see how your child is doing in PE. The platform is built so you can self-register — no school staff intervention required — and view your child's progress in plain English.

Self-registering

You'll need: your child's first name, last name, and student ID (sometimes called "local student identifier"). Your school's office can give you the ID if you don't have it.

1
Open https://ufitmotion.onrender.com/login.
2
Click Parent Portal (the family icon).
3
Click Create Account below the Sign In form.
4
Step 1 — Verify your child: enter your child's first name, last name, and student ID exactly as shown on school records. Click Continue.
5
If verified, you'll see "✓ Verified — [Child's Name], Grade X, [School]". If not, double-check spelling or contact your school's office.
6
Step 2 — Your information: your name, email, phone, relationship (Mother / Father / Guardian / etc.), and a password.
7
Click Create Account. You're auto-logged-in and land on your child's progress view.
Both parents already registered? If your child already has two parents in the system, your registration completes but stays unlinked. The school office gets a notification to resolve it. Email operations@ufitonline.net if you need help.

Viewing progress

The progress view is read-only. There's nothing for you to fill in — Ufit's coaches do the data entry.

  • Overall UFIT score (0-100) with a performance band: Emerging / Developing / On Track / Proficient / Advanced.
  • Top strength — your child's highest-scoring skill.
  • Top growth — biggest improvement from baseline.
  • Attendance — how many sessions your child attended.
  • Behavior readiness — six-dimension rating: teamwork, effort, self-control, listening, sportsmanship, confidence.

Skill scores use a 1-5 rubric (Emerging → Advanced) which the platform converts to 20 / 40 / 60 / 80 / 100 for averaging. Growth = current − baseline.

Account management

Update your contact info

Settings → My Profile → edit name, email, phone → Save.

Change password

Settings → Change Password → enter current + new (8+ chars).

Multiple children?

Once you've registered with one child, you can verify a second from your dashboard: My Children → + Add Another Child → repeat the verification flow.

Forgot password

Login screen → "Forgot password?" → enter email. Reset link arrives in ~30 seconds.
